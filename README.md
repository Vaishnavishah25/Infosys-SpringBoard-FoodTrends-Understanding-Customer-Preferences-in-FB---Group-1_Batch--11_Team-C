## â­ Key Features
- *Market Presence & Coverage Analysis*
  - Country-wise and city-wise restaurant distribution
  - Identification of major restaurant chains and expansion hotspots

- *Cuisine Demand & Popularity Analysis*
  - Popular cuisines by restaurant count
  - Cuisine-wise customer engagement using votes and ratings
  - Interactive slicers for dynamic exploration

- *Customer Engagement & Rating Behavior*
  - Rated vs Not Rated restaurant comparison
  - Relationship between votes, ratings, and online delivery availability
  - City-level customer engagement insights

- *Pricing Strategy & Customer Perception*
  - Price range categorization (Low, Medium, High, Premium)
  - Impact of pricing on customer ratings
  - Currency-based cost analysis using slicers

- *Service Availability & Convenience Impact*
  - Online delivery and table booking analysis
  - City and country-wise service adoption trends

- *Restaurant Consistency & Quality Stability*
  - Vote bucket analysis to assess rating reliability
  - Identification of high-rating but low-vote risk restaurants

- *Strategic Action & Restaurant Prioritization*
  - Action categories such as:
    - Promote (Trusted Performer)
    - Boost Visibility (Hidden Gem)
    - Improve Quality (Reputation Risk)
    - Monitor / Low Priority
  - Clear, actionable recommendations for business decision-making
